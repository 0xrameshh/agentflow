Product Launch Checklist

Pre-Launch (T-30 days):
- Finalize feature scope with product team
- Complete QA regression suite (all tests must pass at 100%)
- Security audit by external vendor
- Legal review of Terms of Service and Privacy Policy
- Prepare marketing materials (blog post, social media, email campaign)
- Set up monitoring dashboards (Datadog / Grafana)
- Configure CI/CD pipeline for production deployment
- Load testing: target 10,000 concurrent users with < 500ms p95 latency

Launch Week:
- Deployment window: Tuesday 10:00 AM – 2:00 PM UTC (avoid Mondays/Fridays)
- Rollout: 10% canary → 50% → 100% over 48 hours
- On-call rotation: 3 engineers (primary + secondary + backup)
- Monitor error rates: < 0.1% 5xx errors as target, > 0.5% triggers rollback
- Customer communication: in-app banner + email announcement on launch day
- Support team briefed with known issues FAQ

Post-Launch (T+30 days):
- Retrospective meeting with engineering, product, support
- Review metrics against launch targets
- Collect user feedback via NPS survey
- Prioritize bug fixes and quick wins from feedback
- Celebrate with team!
