Incident Response Runbook

Severity Levels:
- SEV1: Service outage affecting all users, data loss, security breach.
  Response time: 15 minutes. Page entire engineering team.
- SEV2: Feature outage affecting many users (no workaround).
  Response time: 1 hour. Page primary on-call.
- SEV3: Partial feature degradation (workaround available).
  Response time: 4 hours. Standard ticket.
- SEV4: Cosmetic issue, feature request.
  Response time: next business day.

Incident Commander (IC) Duties:
1. Acknowledge the alert within 5 minutes.
2. Declare severity and assemble response team in a dedicated Slack channel (#incident-xxx).
3. Focus on mitigation, NOT root cause analysis during active incident.
4. Communicate status updates every 30 minutes (SEV1/2) or every 2 hours (SEV3).
5. When service is restored, declare "mitigated" and start post-mortem.

Post-Mortem Requirements:
- Timeline of events (detection, response, mitigation, resolution).
- Root cause analysis (5 Whys method).
- Action items with owners and deadlines.
- Post-mortem review within 5 business days of incident end.
