# Remote Work Policy

## Eligibility
All full-time employees are eligible for remote work up to 4 days per week. Contractors and part-time staff may request remote work on a case-by-case basis.

## Expectations
- Remote employees must be available during core hours: 10:00 AM – 3:00 PM local time.
- Response time to Slack/email: within 1 hour during core hours.
- Video calls require a working camera and a quiet environment.
- Internet connection must support video conferencing (minimum 10 Mbps download / 3 Mbps upload).

## Equipment
The company provides:
- Laptop (MacBook Pro or Dell XPS)
- 24" external monitor (upon request)
- Noise-canceling headphones (one-time, budget up to $150)
- Monthly internet stipend of $75

## Home office safety
Employees must complete a self-certified workstation assessment within 30 days of starting remote work. The HR team provides the assessment form.

## Time tracking
Remote employees log hours in the company time-tracking system. Minimum 7.5 hours per day, 37.5 hours per week. Overtime requires manager pre-approval.
