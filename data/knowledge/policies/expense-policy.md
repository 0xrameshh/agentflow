# Company Expense Policy

## Eligible expenses
Employees may claim the following business expenses with manager approval:
- Travel (flights, trains, ride-share): up to $1,500 per trip without VP approval.
- Lodging: up to $250/night domestic, $400/night international.
- Meals: up to $75/day per person ($100/day for international travel).
- Client entertainment: up to $200 per event.
- Office supplies: up to $100 without pre-approval.

## Ineligible expenses
The following are NOT reimbursable:
- Personal groceries or alcohol.
- Parking tickets or traffic violations.
- First-class travel upgrades.
- Gym memberships or wellness programs.

## How to submit
1. Fill out the expense report form in the HR portal.
2. Attach itemized receipts (photos accepted for amounts under $50).
3. Submit within 30 days of the expense date.
4. Manager approval is required for all expenses over $50.

## Reimbursement timeline
Approved expense reports are processed within 5 business days and deposited via direct deposit or PayPal.

## Travel booking policy
All flights must be booked at least 14 days in advance unless the booking is for an emergency. Economy class is the standard; business class requires VP approval for flights over 6 hours.
