# Billing FAQ

FlowDesk offers three subscription plans:

| Plan | Price | Key Features |
|------|-------|-------------|
| Free | $0 | Up to 3 projects, 5 team members, 1GB storage |
| Pro | $29/user/month | Unlimited projects, 50GB storage, priority support |
| Team | $99/user/month | Unlimited everything, SSO, audit logs, 500GB storage |

## How to upgrade
Go to **Settings → Billing → Change Plan**. The new price is prorated for the remaining billing period.

## How to downgrade
From **Settings → Billing → Change Plan**, select a lower tier. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle. You must reduce active projects/team members to fit the new plan's limits before downgrading.

## Failed payments
If your payment method fails, FlowDesk retries up to 3 times over 7 days. After 3 failed attempts, your account is downgraded to Free and you receive a notification. Unpaid invoices appear under **Settings → Billing → Invoice History**.

## Invoices and receipts
All invoices are available under **Settings → Billing → Invoice History**. Invoices are generated on the 1st of each month for the previous billing month. You can download PDF invoices at any time.

## Changing payment method
Navigate to **Settings → Billing → Payment Method**. You can switch between credit/debit cards and PayPal. For annual plans, only credit/debit cards are accepted.

## Annual vs monthly billing
Annual plans offer a 20% discount compared to monthly billing. Annual billing charges the full amount upfront. Refunds for annual plans follow the standard refund policy (see refund-policy.md).

## Questions about billing?
Contact billing@flowdesk.com or escalate via escalation-guide.md.
