# Refund Policy

FlowDesk offers a **30-day refund window** for all new subscriptions.

## Refund eligibility

- **First-time Pro/Team subscriptions:** Full refund within 30 days of purchase.
- **Renewals:** No refunds for renewal charges. You can cancel before the renewal date.
- **Add-ons:** Refundable within 14 days of purchase (e.g., extra storage, additional seats).
- **Annual plans:** Pro-rated refund for the unused portion after the first 30 days, minus a 10% processing fee.

## How to request a refund

1. Go to **Settings → Billing → Request Refund**.
2. Select the subscription or add-on you want refunded.
3. Provide a reason (optional but helps us improve).
4. Submit. Our billing team reviews requests within 2 business days.

Alternatively, contact billing@flowdesk.com with your account email and invoice number.

## Processing time

Refunds are processed within **5–10 business days** after approval. The amount is returned to the original payment method.

## Exceptions

- **Enterprise contracts:** Governed by separate terms — contact your account manager.
- **Promotional purchases:** Non-refundable unless stated otherwise in the promotion terms.
- **Accounts terminated for ToS violation:** Not eligible for refunds.

## Partial refund scenarios

- **Downgrade mid-cycle:** The unused portion of the higher tier is credited as account balance, not refunded. See billing-faq.md for downgrade details.
- **Seat reduction:** Pro-rated credit applied to next invoice.

For escalation, see escalation-guide.md.
