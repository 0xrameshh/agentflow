# Account Settings

Manage your FlowDesk account from **Settings** in the top-right menu.

## Changing your email
1. Go to **Settings → Profile → Email**.
2. Enter your new email and current password.
3. A verification link is sent to the **new** email address.
4. Click the link within 24 hours to confirm the change.
5. Your login email updates immediately after confirmation.

**Note:** You cannot change to an email already registered to another FlowDesk account.

## Deleting your account
1. Go to **Settings → Account → Delete Account**.
2. You'll be asked to confirm — type "DELETE" to proceed.
3. The account is deactivated for **30 days** (grace period).
4. During the grace period, log in to cancel the deletion.
5. After 30 days, all data is permanently erased.

**Before deleting:**
- Export your data first (see "Exporting data" below).
- Cancel any active subscriptions to avoid future charges.
- Transfer workspace ownership if you're the sole owner.

## Exporting data
1. Go to **Settings → Data → Export Data**.
2. Select what to export: Projects, Tasks, Comments, or All.
3. Choose format: CSV or JSON.
4. Click **Request Export**. You'll receive an email when it's ready.
5. Standard exports are available for **7 days** after generation.

For large exports (>1GB), see data-export.md for delivery timelines.

## Workspace roles
Workspaces have three roles:

| Role | Permissions |
|------|------------|
| **Owner** | Full access, billing, member management, deletion |
| **Admin** | Full access except billing and workspace deletion |
| **Member** | Access assigned projects only |

To change a role: **Settings → Workspace → Members → Edit Role**. Only workspace Owners can change roles.

## Notification preferences
1. Go to **Settings → Notifications**.
2. Toggle email, in-app, and mobile push notifications per event type.
3. Configure daily/weekly digest emails under **Digest Settings**.

## Password change
1. Go to **Settings → Security → Change Password**.
2. Enter current password, then new password (min 8 chars, one number, one special character).
3. Click **Save**. You'll be logged out of all active sessions.

For login issues, see login-troubleshooting.md.
